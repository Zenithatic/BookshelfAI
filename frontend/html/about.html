Why BookshelfAI?

My Inspiration

"I dedicate this app to my girlfriend, who has a deep love for books, especially romance novels, and is always on the lookout for new ones. This app simplifies the way she manages her book wishlist by allowing her to easily add new titles using the book image recognition feature, whether she's at a bookstore or anywhere else."

- Patrick, founder of BookshelfAI

The team behind BookshelfAI

At the moment, the only person working on BookshelfAI is me, Patrick Wang. I'm a first year student currently studying computer science at University of Waterloo.

🤷❓

Who this web app is for

This app is for anyone, really, but is specifically tailored to book lovers who want an easy way to manage their own list of books. Feel free to reach out at the Help section in the navigation bar. Disclaimer: this app does not provide any actual book content itself, it only acts as a book manager, not a library.

🛠️🥞

The tech stack

BookshelfAI's frontend is vanilla HTML/CSS/Javascript, with Node.js and express for the backend alongside PostgreSQL for the database. One day, BookshelfAI might be rebuilt with Next.js.
